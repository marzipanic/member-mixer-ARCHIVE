Member Mixer

This tool will help you, the Event Planner, create a schedule for gatherings to be hosted and attended by a pool of people. These gatherings prioritize meeting new people, while providing control for participants' availability.

Important concepts when using this tool include:

  • Group: a collection of participants.
    • Host: a group that is responsible for hosting a gathering
    • Attendee: a group that is attending a gathering
  • Gathering: a meeting that is hosted by a single group and attended by some (but not all) other groups
  • Event: a date/time during which several gatherings may be held

To get started, fill out the following items in order:

  1. Settings
  2. Events
  3. Groups
When you are done, click the "Generate Itinerary" button at the bottom of the page.
